Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your iPhone. No servers, no cloud database. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account — only you can access it.

Does SnapCents connect to my bank?

No. You add transactions by scanning receipts, voice entry, manual entry, or importing PDF bank statements — all processed on your device.

How does the AI assistant work without internet?

SnapCents uses Apple Foundation Models running entirely on your device. Your financial data is never sent to any server.

Is SnapCents really free?

Yes. The free tier includes core features with some limits. Pro unlocks more when you're ready. No ads, no data selling — ever.

Can I track business and personal expenses?

Yes. Separate profiles with instant switching. Each profile has its own transactions, budgets, and reports.

How much does Pro cost?

$3.99/month, $29.99/year (with 7-day free trial), or $74.99 lifetime.
